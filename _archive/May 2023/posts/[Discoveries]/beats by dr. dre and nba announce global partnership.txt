# [Beats by Dr. Dre and NBA Announce Global Partnership](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20180905005755/en/Beats-Dr.-Dre-NBA-Announce-Global-Partnership)

> Beats by Dr. Dre (Beats), the leading global premium headphone brand co-founded by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine, and the National Basketball Association (NBA) today announced a comprehensive marketing and merchandising partnership that will make the company the official headphone, wireless speaker and audio partner of the NBA, Women’s National Basketball Association (WNBA), NBA G League and USA Basketball beginning in October 2018.

Perfect fit for two brands that are everywhere in culture.

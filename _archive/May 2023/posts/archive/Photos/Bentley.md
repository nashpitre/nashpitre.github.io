## The Mission

Team up with my girlfriend and give Bentley $40 from both of us, and do it in a creative way. Giving him money alone would be boring. I want to make it epic.

---- 

## Supplies

- Index cards
- Red permanent marker
- Hole puncher
- A picture from 1992
- Red Ribbon
- Silver bow
- Double sided tape
- 20 one dollar bills
- A 20 dollar bill
